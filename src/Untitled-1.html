Khmer[edit]

\n

Etymology[edit]

\n

From Sanskrit विद्या (vidyā, “learning; knowledge; wisdom”). Doublet of វិជ្ជា (vɨcciə). Compare with Thai วิทยา (wít-tá-yaa).\n
